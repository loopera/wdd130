Purpose

Purpose: To make it easier for people to do bookings for whitewater rafting and also to raise the profile of the whitewater rafting business.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Young adults and Adults between 16-60. What do they want? Looking for quality time outdoors. What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? How to book, pricing, and contact information.
